TheHopper is your ultimate karaoke companion app.

🎵 FIND KARAOKE NEAR YOU
Discover karaoke nights at venues near you with location-based search. See who's hosting, what time it starts, and get directions.

🎤 SONG SUGGESTIONS FOR YOUR VOICE
Enter your vocal range (bass, baritone, tenor, alto, mezzo, soprano) and your favorite artists. TheHopper's smart matching algorithm suggests songs that fit your voice and style — so you always sound your best.

⚡ SKIP THE QUEUE
Want to sing next? Pay your KJ (karaoke host) directly through the app to jump the queue. Secure Stripe checkout, no cash needed. Support your local KJ while getting your moment in the spotlight.

⭐ SAVE YOUR FAVORITES
Build your personal songbook. Save songs you love, track what you've sung, and get better suggestions over time. No login required — everything saves right on your device.
